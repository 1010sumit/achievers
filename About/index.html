What led us to build Achievers?

Growing up we haven't had the luxury of studying in an institution that gives equal importance to personality development and academic knowledge. Unfortunately, in our institution, Personality Development was reduced to a high-scoring subject that helps you offset the low marks in other disciplines.
Nonetheless, as we entered college, we were encouraged to participate in different personality development activities and there we realized how important and life-changing it can be. That first-hand experience was the aha movement that sparked the idea of building a 360-degree personality development solution for all age groups. After several cups of coffee, intense brainstorming sessions, and hours of coding, Achievers was ready to go live on the internet.
